SAE J1939 support

The CANopen Master protocol stack can support the SAE J1939 protocol. Setting the flag eCOM_MODE_J1939 during the initialisation (refer to ComMgrInit() function) enables the J1939 mode.

  • address claiming and NAME
  • adding PGNs
  • BAM and CMDT
